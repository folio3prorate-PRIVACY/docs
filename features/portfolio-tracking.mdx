# Portfolio Tracking

Octav provides comprehensive portfolio tracking for all your digital assets.

## Multi-Chain Support

Track assets across all major blockchains:

- Ethereum
- Base
- Arbitrum
- Optimism
- Polygon
- Solana
- And many more...

## Real-Time Updates

Your portfolio data is updated in real-time, ensuring you always have the most current information about your holdings.

<Alert type="success" title="Automatic Syncing">
Octav automatically syncs your wallet data every few minutes to keep your portfolio up-to-date.
</Alert>

## Supported Asset Types

- **Tokens** (ERC-20, SPL, etc.)
- **NFTs** (ERC-721, ERC-1155)
- **DeFi Positions** (Lending, staking, liquidity pools)
- **Native Assets** (ETH, SOL, etc.)

## Portfolio Views

Customize how you view your portfolio:

### By Asset
See all your holdings grouped by token or asset type.

### By Chain
View your assets organized by blockchain.

### By Protocol
Track your positions across DeFi protocols.

### By Wallet
Monitor individual wallet performance.

## Historical Tracking

Octav maintains a complete history of your portfolio:

- Daily snapshots of portfolio value
- Transaction history
- Performance over custom time periods
- Asset allocation changes over time
