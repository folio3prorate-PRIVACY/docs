# Welcome to Octav

Octav provides portfolio intelligence for digital assets, turning complex blockchain data into clear, actionable insights.

## What is Octav?

Octav is a comprehensive portfolio management and analytics platform designed for digital asset investors and managers. Track your holdings, monitor performance, and gain deep insights into your crypto portfolio.

### Key Features

- **Real-time Portfolio Tracking** - Monitor all your digital assets in one place
- **Advanced Analytics** - Comprehensive performance metrics and insights
- **Multi-chain Support** - Track assets across multiple blockchains
- **Tax & Compliance** - Automated reporting and compliance tools
