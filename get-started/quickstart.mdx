# Quickstart Guide

Get up and running with Octav in just a few minutes.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Sign up for Octav at [octav.fi](https://octav.fi) to get started.

<Alert type="info" title="Free Trial Available">
Start with a free trial to explore all of Octav's features.
</Alert>

## Step 2: Connect Your Wallets

Connect your digital asset wallets to Octav to start tracking your portfolio.

1. Navigate to the **Wallets** section
2. Click **Add Wallet**
3. Enter your wallet address or connect via WalletConnect
4. Confirm the connection

## Step 3: View Your Portfolio

Once your wallets are connected, Octav automatically:

- Fetches all your token balances
- Calculates current values
- Tracks historical performance
- Generates analytics and insights

## Step 4: Explore Analytics

Dive into your portfolio analytics:

- **Performance Charts** - Track your portfolio value over time
- **Asset Breakdown** - See your holdings by asset, chain, or protocol
- **P&L Reports** - Understand your profit and loss
- **Tax Reports** - Generate reports for tax compliance
