## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I report a bug?
1. Check if it's a known issue in [Discord](https://discord.gg/qvcknAz73A)
2. Email support@octav.fi with:
   - Description of the issue
   - Steps to reproduce
   - Screenshots if applicable
   - Browser/device information

### How do I request a feature?
Visit our [Submit a Feature](/docs/submit-feature) page to submit feature requests.

### What are your support hours?
- **Discord:** Community available 24/7, team active during business hours (PST)
- **Email Support:** Monitored during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM PST, Monday-Friday)
- **Emergency/Critical Issues:** Contact support@octav.fi with "URGENT" in subject line

### Do you offer enterprise support?
Yes! Enterprise and team plans include:
- Priority support
- Dedicated account manager
- SLA guarantees
- Custom integrations
- Training sessions

Contact partnerships@octav.fi for enterprise inquiries.

## Report Security Issues

<Alert type="danger" title="Security Vulnerability">
If you've discovered a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly.
</Alert>

### Security Contact
- **Email:** security@octav.fi
- **PGP Key:** [Download public key](https://octav.fi/pgp)

**Do not:**
- Publicly disclose the vulnerability
- Share with others before we've addressed it

**We commit to:**
- Acknowledge your report within 24 hours
- Keep you updated on our progress
- Credit you in our security acknowledgments (if desired)
- Potentially offer bug bounties for significant findings

[Learn more about our security program](https://octav.fi/security)
