## Cost Basis Methods

<Warning>
  Consult with a tax professional to determine the appropriate cost basis method for your situation.
</Warning>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="FIFO" icon="arrow-right">

    ### First In, First Out

    - Assumes oldest assets sold first
    - Most common method
    - Often required by default

    **Best For:** Simple tracking and most jurisdictions

  </Tab>

  <Tab title="LIFO" icon="arrow-left">

    ### Last In, First Out

    - Assumes newest assets sold first
    - May reduce tax burden in bull markets

    **Best For:** Reducing gains in rising markets

  </Tab>

  <Tab title="HIFO" icon="arrow-up">

    ### Highest In, First Out

    - Sells highest cost basis first
    - Minimizes taxable gains
    - Requires detailed tracking

    **Best For:** Tax optimization

  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Specific ID" icon="fingerprint">

    ### Specific Identification

    - Manually specify which lots are sold
    - Maximum control
    - Most complex to manage

    **Best For:** Advanced users with complex portfolios

  </Tab>
</Tabs>
